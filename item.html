Driving Laws

Learn NC driving laws: speed limits, right of way, seat belts, child safety, licensing rules, penalties.

Q: What is the maximum speed limit inside a city?
A: 35 mph unless otherwise posted.

Q: Who has the right of way at a 4-way stop?
A: The first vehicle to arrive, or the vehicle on the right if arriving at the same time.

Traffic Signs

Study regulatory, warning, and guide signs. Recognize their colors and shapes.

STOP Sign: Red octagon → complete stop.

YIELD Sign: Red/white triangle → slow and give way.

Safe Driving

Defensive driving, sharing the road, driving in weather, work zones, and night driving tips.

  • Keep 2-second following distance.
  • Check mirrors every 5–8 seconds.
  • Slow down in poor weather.
  • Be cautious in construction zones.

Alcohol & Drugs

NC laws on BAC, penalties, and zero tolerance under 21.

  • 0.08% BAC is the legal limit for drivers over 21.
  • Under 21: any alcohol is illegal (0.00% required).
  • DWI penalties include fines, license suspension, and possible jail time.
